About Kuantum

Kuantum is a new seminar series based in Karlsruhe, bringing together researchers to explore cutting-edge topics in quantum mechanics, spectral theory, and related fields. Our mission is to foster dialogue and inspire collaboration in a welcoming and engaging environment.
