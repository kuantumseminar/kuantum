About Kuantum

Kuantum is a seminar series in Karlsruhe bringing together researchers and students working on quantum mechanics, spectral theory, mathematical physics, and related areas.

This webpage is organized semester by semester: the current semester is displayed here on the main page, while previous schedules remain available in the archive for documentation and continuity.
